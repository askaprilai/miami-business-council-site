About Us

Meet the visionary leaders driving innovation and excellence in Miami's premier business community

April Sabral

Executive Director

April Sabral brings over two decades of transformational leadership experience to the Miami Business Council. As a visionary executive, she has consistently driven organizational excellence across diverse industries, from luxury retail to cutting-edge AI technology.

Her unique blend of strategic acumen and operational expertise makes her the ideal leader to guide our mission of fostering innovation, collaboration, and sustainable growth within Miami's premier business destination.

April's commitment to empowering businesses and building meaningful partnerships aligns perfectly with our vision of establishing Miami's business community as a globally recognized hub of creativity and commerce.

Lubna Najjar

Board President
Financial services professional with expertise in investment strategy, risk management, and sustainable business development practices.
Lubna Najjar is an accomplished entrepreneur, brand developer, and fashion designer, with over a decade of experience in the fashion and brand development industries. As the founder of IL Moda Brand, LLC (2012-present) and Her Denim, LLC, she has played a pivotal role in building and launching over 150 fashion brands, focusing on contemporary and influencer-led labels. Lubna was the curator and developer of the official Fashion District in downtown Columbus, Ohio, showcasing her ability to shape and drive industry initiatives on a large scale. Lubna now serves on the Board of Directors for the Miami Business Council in Miami, FL, and BBB of South East Florida serving 240,000 businesses.

Scott Largen, Esq.

Board Member
Attorney specializing in E-Discovery and mass toxic tort litigation, bringing extensive legal expertise and technology proficiency to the board.
J. Scott Largen is from Richmond, Virginia and received his Bachelor of Science degree in Finance from Virginia Tech. After graduating with honors, he worked in the investment consulting industry in Arlington, Virginia as a performance reporting analyst, serving institutional investor clients including private family offices, universities, pension plans, and international charities. He then attended the University of Miami School of Law where he was a recipient of the Dean's Merit Scholarship. Mr. Largen leads in-house E-Discovery teams for mass toxic tort litigation and has worked extensively with Relativity and Reveal review platforms, holding certifications for Reveal and Brainspace. His unique combination of finance, legal expertise, and technology proficiency provides valuable strategic insight to the council's operations and governance.
