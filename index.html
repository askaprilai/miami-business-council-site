Our Vision

To establish the Miami business community as a globally recognized hub of innovation, collaboration, and ethical business excellence, where vibrant partnerships and community engagement create a thriving, sustainable economic landscape for generations to come.

Our Mission

The mission of the Miami Business Council is to empower businesses to thrive by serving as a vital resource for strategic growth, and by advancing the business community as a global luxury and cultural destination through marketing, advocacy, partnerships, and education.

Professional Networking

Community Impact
