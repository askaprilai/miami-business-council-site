About Us

Meet the visionary leaders driving innovation and excellence in Miami's Design District

April Sabral

Executive Director

April Sabral brings over two decades of transformational leadership experience to the Miami Design Chamber of Commerce. As a visionary executive, she has consistently driven organizational excellence across diverse industries, from luxury retail to cutting-edge AI technology.

Her unique blend of strategic acumen and operational expertise makes her the ideal leader to guide our chamber's mission of fostering innovation, collaboration, and sustainable growth within Miami's premier design destination.

April's commitment to empowering businesses and building meaningful partnerships aligns perfectly with our vision of establishing the Design District as a globally recognized hub of creativity and commerce.
